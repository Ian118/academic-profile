QUAD was a project in [TURTLE](https://turtlerobotics.org), an undergraduate robotics student organization at Texas A&M, to develop a series of quadrupedal robots and explore the capabilities of legged robotics. I was the lead of this project for 3 years, and developed 4 revisions of the QUAD robotic dog.
