As a deliverable for MEEN-408 _Mechanics of Robotic Manipulators_, I designed and built a single degree of freedom robotic gripper. The objective of the project was to design a simple robotic gripper with a single degree of freedom in actuation as an end-effector for the UR3e robotic arm.
